VII. LIMITATIONS

1. Context Fragmentation

The model is limited to read 512 tokens at once

Long reports must be split into chunks, leading to loss of context and a potential drop in overall performance
2. Annotation Noise

Expert's annotations require refinement

The failure to completely filter out outliers indicates that the dataset still contains some noise

VIII. MY TAKEAWAYS

Insights & Critical Thinking

1. Beyond Images
2. Open-Source
3. Pre-training Bias?

1. Text as Foundation

Great significance lies in focusing on the foundational text (reports) to extract clinical context, moving beyond solely relying on image-based diagnostics.

2. Community Impact

Accelerates medical AI research by making this massive, expert-annotated dataset and model publicly available (Open-Source).

3. Data Leakage?

Is the high performance of baseline partly due to its prior exposure to CXP during the pre-training phase?
